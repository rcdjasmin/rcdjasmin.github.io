Redesigning Ascenda’s corporate website

One of my first major projects at Ascenda was a redesign of our marketing site. The company had shifted strategy completely since the last refresh three years ago—from focusing only on high-level impact to actually showcasing our product and features in detail.


The deadline was tight. With our biggest lead-generating conference just 3 months away, we needed to get the new site launched beforehand. I worked alongside our creative director and motion designer to bring this project live just in time.
