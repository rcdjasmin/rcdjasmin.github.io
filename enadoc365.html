Enadoc 365 Mobile App

At Tech One Global, I led the design of a new mobile scanning app called Enadoc 365. While there are countless scanning apps in the market, we aim to set this one apart with its fresh and straightforward process.

ENADOC 365 IN THE APP STORE

Branding

Enadoc 365, derived from an existing brand called Enadoc, is the personal and consumer-centric counterpart of this enterprise app. The brand identity reflected this with friendlier colors, typography, illustrations, and custom icons.

Wireframes & Prototypes

Lo-fi prototypes made with sketches on paper that are stitched in Marvel were used to explore our different navigation options. Afterwards, we moved on to creating the wireframes in Sketch, increasing in fidelity with each version.
